The Unofficial Elder Scrolls Mud!

Welcome to the UESM. Set ten years after the Oblivion Crisis of 3E433, the year is 4E10 the reign on the Septim dynasty has ended as the state of Tamriel lays in complete disarry recovering from the carnage of Dagon's forces.

The world of Mundus is large and there is much work to be done. This is a personal project of mine, as I am a huge fan of the Elder Scrolls and sad to see that it hasn't been created yet. This is an undertaking that I have had on the back of my mind for over a decade now. The game rules is based of a slightly modified version of Unofficial Elder Scrolls Role Playing Game 3e

The status of the game is in pre-alpha and designed to resemble a playing style similiar to Diku and ROM muds. The code is open-source> for those who would like contribute to the code. Click here to see the current roadmap of the project.
